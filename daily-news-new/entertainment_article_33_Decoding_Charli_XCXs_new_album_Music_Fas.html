Charli XCX was supposed to be taking a break.

Son sixième album, Brat, était le genre de disque que l’on pourrait véritablement qualifier de définissant une époque, sans être accusé d’en exagérer l’importance.

Her sixth album, Brat, was the sort of record you could genuinely call era-defining, without being accused of overstating its significance.

Des chansons expérimentales mais accessibles comme Guess, Apple et 360 ont propulsé la star vers de nouveaux sommets de gloire.

Experimental but accessible songs like Guess, Apple and 360 propelled the star to new heights of fame.

Son album a été coopté dans la campagne présidentielle de Kamala Harris en 2024, et le Collins English Dictionary a mis à jour sa définition de Brat, pour reconnaître son utilisation comme adjectif caractérisant « l'attitude confiante, indépendante et hédoniste » de la musique.

Her album was co-opted into Kamala Harris's 2024 presidential campaign, and Collins English Dictionary updated its definition of Brat, to acknowledge its use as an adjective characterising the music's "confident, independent, and hedonistic attitude".

Après une tournée éprouvante d'un an, la chanteuse a accepté quelques rôles d'acteur, a écrit la bande originale de l'adaptation de Wuthering Heights par Emerald Fennel et a épousé son partenaire de longue date George Daniel.

After a punishing year-long tour, the singer accepted a few acting roles, wrote the soundtrack to Emerald Fennel's adaptation of Wuthering Heights and married her longterm partner George Daniel.

Mais au lieu de s’installer, elle s’est retrouvée en studio.

But instead of settling down, she found herself back in the studio.

"Out of the blue, inspiration struck me," she says. "I suddenly felt like I had something to say, so I ran with it."

En quelques semaines, elle a composé 11 courtes chansons à base de guitare qui tentaient de donner un sens à son tourbillon de deux années.

In a matter of weeks, she dashed off 11 short, guitar-based songs that tried to make sense of her whirlwind two years.

L’album qui en résulte, intitulé Music, Fashion, Film, est intelligent, autoréférentiel, drôle, antagoniste et regorge de moments pour stimuler votre ver d’oreille intérieur.

The resulting album, titled Music, Fashion, Film, is smart, self-referential, funny, antagonistic and crammed full of moments to stimulate your inner earworm.

De manière significative, délibérément, ce n'est pas Brat 2.0.

Significantly, deliberately, it's not Brat 2.0.

In the aforementioned Vogue article, Charli played the interviewer a track called Rock Music, featuring the lyric: "I think the dancefloor is dead, so now we're making rock music".

La publication a pris cela comme un évangile, qualifiant la musique, la mode et le cinéma de « réinvention du rock » de la star.

The publication took that as gospel, calling Music, Fashion, Film the star's "rock reinvention".

But while guitars are the album's predominant sound, it's far from being a rejection of club music. Even the song Rock Music is full of manipulated vocals and electronic flourishes.

"That's shaped who I am as an artist. [Rock Music] is kind of about my pivot from that as an artist who doesn't want to continue to do the same thing over and over and over again."

The trio epitomise excellence in their respective fields - reflecting the album's overarching theme of art, excellence and immortality.

On Rock Music, Charli admits that the head-banging physicality of the Brat tour ended up giving her nerve damage in her neck.

The album explores the tension between her public persona and her private life

Charli's new songs find her grappling with whether she can stay true to her pugnacious, subversive roots while enjoying commercial success and personal happiness.

Le single slinky SS26 discute de la pression exercée sur les pop stars pour qu'elles fassent et disent les bonnes choses.

The slinky single SS26 discusses the pressure on pop stars to do and say the right things.

En paroles, Charli désavoue les cigarettes et écrit des excuses sincères pour ses erreurs passées. Elle parle même d'utiliser sa politique comme une « stratégie de presse », notant que son héritage en partie indien « pourrait me donner une sacrée USP ».

Lyrically, Charli disavows cigarettes and writes sincere apologies for her past mistakes. She even talks about using her politic as a "press strategy", noting that her part-Indian heritage "could give me quite the USP".

Mêlé de sarcasme, il met en lumière l'idiotie de maintenir les célébrités à des normes inaccessibles, au lieu de laisser la musique (et la mode et le cinéma) explorer le côté le plus sombre de la nature humaine.

Laced with sarcasm, it highlights the idiocy of holding celebrities to unattainable standards, instead of letting music (and fashion and film) explore the darker side of human nature.

Si les paroles de Brat étaient directes et sans fioritures, le nouvel album de Charli pousse le minimalisme à l'extrême.

If the lyrics on Brat were direct and unembellished, Charli's new album takes minimalism to an extreme.

La septième chanson, Yeah, est un exemple classique de tension et de relâchement dans la musique pop - qui réduit l'idée à sa forme la plus élémentaire.

The seventh song, Yeah, is a textbook example of tension and release in pop music - that strips the idea down to its most elemental form.

Pendant les 30 premières secondes, Charli répète simplement la phrase « Je ne veux pas » encore et encore tandis qu'une guitare résonne en arrière-plan.

For the first 30 seconds, Charli simply repeats the phrase, "I don't wanna," over and over while a guitar chugs in the background.

Yeah is the most demo-like song on a 29-minute album that occasionally feels under-cooked. But the brevity was quite deliberate.
